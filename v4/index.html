v4 keeps the v3 minimalist style and the two switchers, but reworks the flows so the app explains itself. Less to read, fewer dead-ends, every state in plain language.

What changed for UX
  • • Records opens with a status summary — “8 of 10 will reach a trustee”, 2 trustees, next check-in — so you grasp your vault at a glance.
  • • A gentle nudge surfaces gaps (a record with no trustee) with a one-tap fix.
  • • Plain-language statuses (“Will reach 2”, “No trustee”, “Needs master password”) instead of coloured dots.
  • • Actions are always visible (no hover-only buttons) and adding a record is two fields.
