How it works

Four quiet steps. Then it just waits.

You set it up once. Testamently looks after the rest — and only ever acts if you stop responding.

📥
STEP 1

Add what matters

Store the codes, locations and messages your loved ones will need. Encrypted the moment you type.

👥
STEP 2

Choose who gets it

Assign a trustee to each record. They’re invited and verified — but can’t see anything yet.

🔄
STEP 3

We check in with you

Every so often we ask you to confirm you’re OK. A single tap, and everything stays private.

🛡️
STEP 4

Released, only if needed

If your check-ins ever stop, your trustees get exactly what you left them — never before.

🔒 You stay in control the whole time. Edit, re-assign or delete anything — and one tap resets the clock.
