The problem

When someone dies, the access dies with them.

The information needed to settle a life is scattered across phones, safes and people’s heads — and most of it is simply lost. Existing tools ask for the one thing nobody wants to upload: live passwords.

The insight

Store the half that’s useless to a thief.

People won’t trust an app with their live bank password. They will trust it with the safe combination or the phone PIN — because alone, those open nothing. That single reframe unlocks the market and gives us a security claim no password manager can make.
