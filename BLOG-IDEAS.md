# Testamently — Content & blog direction

## The shift
The **old** blog was about **encryption / security tech**. That's no longer the story — encryption
is table-stakes now. The **new** blog is about the **use of the app: real situations, how much it
helps, what would otherwise be lost.** Emotional + practical, not technical.

**Rules**
- **One idea per article** (one use-case, one "what if", one thing people forget).
- Lead with the *human moment*, not the feature. Show the value, mention the product lightly.
- Tie back to the model where natural: *you hold half · released only if your check-ins stop ·
  not a will, but points to it.*
- Plain, warm, reassuring — never fear-mongering.

## Article ideas (1 per idea)

### 🧭 "What would otherwise be lost" (the strongest hook)
1. **The £2bn of life insurance no one claims** — and how a single note prevents it.
2. **~20% of all Bitcoin is lost forever** — keys that died with their owner. Don't be a statistic.
3. **The Swiss account your family will never find** — quietly recording what exists.
4. **The pension you forgot you had** — old workplace schemes that go unclaimed.
5. **Where's the will?** — why "I made a will" isn't enough if no one can find it.
6. **The safe-deposit box problem** — the bank won't tell them it exists.

### 🔐 "The half they'll need" (access, the wedge)
7. **Don't store your bank password — store the safe combination.** The split-knowledge idea, plainly.
8. **Your phone holds 20 years of photos. What happens to the passcode?**
9. **Crypto inheritance done right:** the PIN + where the wallet is — *never* the seed phrase.
10. **The spare key, the storage unit, the garage code** — the boring stuff that strands families.

### 🤍 "The things that aren't worth money" (sentimental — give this equal weight)
11. **A letter your children open one day** — how to write the one that matters.
12. **Where are the family photos, really?** — drives, clouds, and the logins that lock them away.
13. **The story behind the ring** — passing on meaning, not just objects.
14. **Last wishes, in your own words** — funeral, music, who to tell.

### 🛟 "Peace of mind / how it works" (product-led, gentle)
15. **The 5-minute version of getting your affairs in order** — start with one record.
16. **A dead-man's switch, explained kindly** — what "if you stop checking in" really means.
17. **Choosing a trustee:** who, how many, and what they can (and can't) see.
18. **Why we built something that's useless if we're hacked** — security as architecture, told as a story.

### 🗓️ Seasonal / occasion hooks
19. **New year, one quiet task** — the resolution that actually protects your family.
20. **A gift that isn't a thing** — setting it up *with* a parent.

## SEO / distribution notes
- Target intent like *"unclaimed life insurance," "crypto inheritance," "where to keep my will,"
  "what happens to my photos when I die,"* "digital legacy."
- Each post → 1 matching **social graphic** (see `/social/`) + a soft CTA to *how-it-works*.
- Repurpose: article #7/#9/#16 map directly to the existing social posts.
